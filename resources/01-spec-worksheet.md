# The Seven Questions · Spec Worksheet
# 七个问题 · 产品规格问答表

**Day 3, morning.** Print one per team. Answer by hand.
**第三天上午。** 每组一份，手写作答。

> Seven questions — not a template. Nobody is intimidated by seven questions.
> They're intimidated by "write a Product Requirements Document."
>
> 这是七个问题，不是一张表格。没有人会怕回答七个问题，
> 但很多人会怕"写一份产品需求文档"。

---

**Team / 小组：** ___________________  **Date / 日期：** ___________

---

### 1. Who is this for?
### 这是做给谁用的？

Name an actual person you met on Day 2. Not "rural students" — a person.
写下第二天你真正见到的一个人的名字。不要写"乡村学生"，要写一个具体的人。

<br><br>

---

### 2. What is their problem, in one sentence?
### 他们的问题是什么？用一句话说清楚。

Quote the interview if you can.
如果可以，直接引用访谈里的原话。

<br><br>

---

### 3. Finish this sentence.
### 把这句话补完。

> You open our app, you ________________, and you get ________________.
> 你打开我们的应用，你 ________________，然后你得到 ________________。

<br><br>

---

### 4. What is the ONE thing it does?
### 它做的那 **一件** 事是什么？

One. Not three. If you need the word "and," you have two features — pick one.
一件。不是三件。如果你的句子里出现了"并且"，说明你有两个功能——挑一个。

<br><br>

---

### 5. What must the app remember?
### 这个应用必须记住什么？

Plain English. A list of things, not columns — the table design is tomorrow's job.
用大白话列出来。列一串"东西"，不是列"字段"——建表是明天的事。

<br><br>

---

### 6. What must it never do?
### 它绝对不能做什么？

These become your Constitution this afternoon.
这些内容会在今天下午变成你们的"宪法"。

<br><br>

---

### 7. What are we NOT building — and how will we know it worked?
### 我们**不做**什么？以及，怎么算成功？

Out of scope, plus one test a real user could pass or fail.
写下不做的范围，再写一条真实用户可以通过或失败的检验标准。

<br><br>

---

## Before you hand this in / 交上来之前

- [ ] It fits on one page. **If it doesn't fit, you don't understand it yet.**
      能装进一页纸。**装不下，说明你还没想清楚。**
- [ ] Question 4 names exactly one feature.
      第 4 题只写了一个功能。
- [ ] Question 1 names a real person, not a category.
      第 1 题写的是一个真人，不是一类人。
- [ ] Question 7 has a test someone could actually run.
      第 7 题的标准是别人真的能拿去测的。
