# The AI-Interviewer Prompt
# AI 采访官提示词

**Day 3.** Instead of writing a spec from a blank page, let the AI interview *you*.
**第三天。** 与其对着空白页写规格，不如让 AI 反过来采访你。

---

## Copy this into your AI / 把下面这段复制给 AI

```
You are an experienced product manager. I am a high-school student building a
small AI web app for rural students in Jiangxi.

Interview me one question at a time — wait for my answer before asking the next.
Ask about the user, their real problem, the single core feature, what the app
must remember, and what we should deliberately not build.

When you have enough, write it up as a one-page product spec. Keep it plain and
short. Do not invent facts about the users; if you need something, ask me.
```

**中文版（如果你更习惯用中文思考）：**

```
你是一位经验丰富的产品经理。我是一名高中生，正在为江西的乡村学生
做一个小型 AI 网页应用。

请一次只问我一个问题——等我回答完再问下一个。围绕以下几点提问：
用户是谁、他们真正的问题、唯一的核心功能、应用必须记住什么、
以及我们应该刻意不做什么。

等你了解得差不多了，把它整理成一页纸的产品规格。语言要朴素、简短。
不要编造关于用户的事实；需要什么信息，直接问我。
```

---

## Then the part that actually matters / 接下来才是重点

**The AI drafts. You decide.**
**AI 负责起草，你负责决定。**

It will pad. It will over-generalise. It will add features nobody asked for and
write sentences that sound impressive but mean nothing.

它会啰嗦，会说套话，会加上没人要求过的功能，会写出一些听起来很厉害、
其实什么也没说的句子。

**Cutting it back is the lesson.** Go through its draft and:
**删减，才是这堂课的重点。** 拿到它的草稿后：

- Delete every feature that isn't the one core feature
  删掉所有不属于那个"唯一核心功能"的东西
- Replace every vague phrase with something checkable
  把每一句含糊的话，换成可以检验的说法
- Cut it until it fits on one page
  一直删到能装进一页纸为止

---

## Warning / 注意

If the AI states a fact about your users that you did not tell it —
**it made that up.** You met these students on Day 2. The model did not.

如果 AI 说出了任何你没告诉过它的、关于用户的"事实"——
**那是它编的。** 第二天见过这些学生的人是你，不是模型。
