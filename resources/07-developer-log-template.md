# Developer Log
# 开发者日志

**The last ten minutes of every day.** One entry per day, per team.
**每天最后十分钟。** 每组每天一条。

This is not busywork. On Day 7 this log becomes part of your portfolio — and the
ethics case you submit is built almost entirely out of these entries. Write it while
you still remember what actually happened.

这不是走过场。到第七天，这份日志会成为你作品集的一部分——你要提交的伦理案例，
几乎完全是从这些记录里长出来的。趁你还记得发生了什么，就写下来。

---

## Template / 模板

```
### Day _____    Team _____    Date _____

**What we got working today**
今天跑通了什么


**What broke, and what we did about it**
什么坏了？我们怎么处理的？


**Where the AI was wrong**
AI 在哪里出错了？
(Be specific. "It hallucinated" is not an entry. What exactly did it claim,
 and how did you catch it?)
(要具体。"它幻觉了"不算一条记录。它到底说了什么？你是怎么发现的？)


**What we changed our minds about**
我们改变了什么想法？


**One thing we'd do differently tomorrow**
明天有一件事我们会换个做法

```

## Day-specific prompts / 每天的重点

Use these instead of the generic questions when they apply:
以下几天，用这些问题替代上面的通用问题：

**Day 4** — What did the agent get wrong? What did you change in `CLAUDE.md` or your
Skill to fix it?
第四天 —— 智能体哪里做错了？你在 `CLAUDE.md` 或 Skill 里改了什么来修正它？

**Day 5** — Where did the AI explain itself honestly, and where did it bluff?
Which attack broke your product? Which Article did it break?
第五天 —— AI 在哪里如实说明了自己，在哪里是在编？哪次攻击攻破了你的产品？违反了第几条？

**Day 6** — The freeze. The hardest bug the blind test found. The one thing you'd
build next.
第六天 —— 代码冻结。盲测中发现的最难的 bug。如果继续做，你下一步会做什么。

## Why this matters on Day 7 / 为什么第七天你会庆幸写过它

Your ethics case has three parts, and you already have all of them:

你的伦理案例分三部分，而这三部分你其实都已经写好了：

1. **Here were our rules** → your Constitution, Day 3
   **我们定下的规则** → 第三天的宪法
2. **Here is how we were attacked** → your red-team log, Day 5
   **我们是如何被攻击的** → 第五天的红队记录
3. **Here is what held, and what we changed** → these entries
   **哪些守住了，我们又改了什么** → 就是这些日志

The teams who write honest logs — including the parts where they were wrong — write
the best cases. **A log that says "everything worked" is a log nobody believes.**

那些老老实实记录的小组——包括记下自己判断错误的地方——最后写出的案例最好。
**一份"一切顺利"的日志，没有人会相信。**
