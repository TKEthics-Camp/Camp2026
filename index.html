What will I learn你会学到什么

You will walk the full path of a product creator: find a real problem from real users, write it down as a one-page PRD, direct an AI to build it, test it, attack it, publish it — and be able to explain why you made every choice along the way.

Technically, you'll learn vibe coding (driving an AI with clear language), how to store and share code on GitHub, how to use a real database as your backend, and how to write a SKILL.md so the AI does one job reliably instead of randomly.

But the things this course really wants to leave you with are different: learning to question and verify what the AI tells you (it will be confidently wrong), the courage to start before you feel ready, the patience to keep going when it breaks, and the ability to take someone else's criticism and use it as fuel.

你会完整走一遍产品创造者的路：从真实用户身上发现问题，写下一页 PRD， 指挥 AI 把它做出来，测试它、攻击它，最后把它发布出去，并讲清楚自己为什么这样做。

技术上，你会掌握 Vibe Coding——用清晰的语言驱动 AI 编程；学会用 GitHub 保存和分享代码； 用真实的数据库作为后端；给 AI 写 SKILL.md 让它稳定地做好一件事。

但这门课真正想留给你的，是另外几样东西：学会质疑并检验 AI 给的答案（它会一本正经地说错）、 在不确定中敢于开始、在反复失败时保持耐心，以及把别人的批评当作自己前进的燃料。

One thread runs through the whole week: Model → Agent → Workflow. On Day 1, you are the loop — you run the code, you paste the error back in. On Day 4, an AI agent closes that loop by itself. On Day 6, you'll watch several agents decide among themselves who does what.
贯穿全营的一条主线：Model → Agent → Workflow。 第一天你自己是那个循环——你运行代码、你把报错贴回去；第四天，AI 智能体自己完成这个循环； 第六天，你会看到多个智能体自己决定谁该做什么。

Daily notes每日课程

DAY 1 The Attention War, and Learning to Build 注意力战争，与动手搭建 Model

Objectives

Understand how the apps that keep you scrolling were actually designed. Understand why AI says wrong things so confidently. And build your first working app.

Process

  • Icebreaker: "Where do you stand" — vote with your feet, then defend your position
  • Round-robin: how do you actually use AI? Has it ever fooled you?
  • Mini-lecture: how does hallucination happen? — a model predicts the next likely word; it does not look things up
  • Lecture: what is the attention economy? Addiction by design, and how governments have responded
  • Open thinking: mind-map your way to a problem worth solving
  • Tech lecture: what is vibe coding? (the Model → Agent → Workflow map)
  • Build: make a clickable demo with AI
  • Form teams and choose the problem each team will field-test tomorrow

Line of the day

"The AI that invents a fake fact also invents a fake function."

今天的目标

理解那些让人停不下来的 App 究竟是怎么设计的；搞清楚 AI 为什么会一本正经地说错话； 并亲手做出你的第一个能运行的应用。

流程

  • 破冰："你站在哪一边"——用身体投票，为自己的立场辩护
  • 轮流分享：你平时怎么用 AI？它有没有骗过你？
  • 迷你讲座：AI 幻觉是怎么产生的？——它预测下一个最可能的词，而不是查资料
  • 讲座：什么是注意力经济？成瘾式设计，以及各国的监管回应
  • 开放思考：画一张思维导图，找到你想解决的问题
  • 技术讲座：什么是 Vibe Coding？（Model → Agent → Workflow 全景图）
  • 动手搭建：用 AI 做出一个可点击的 demo
  • 组队，并确定每组要去田野验证的问题

今日金句

"会编造假事实的 AI，也一样会编造不存在的函数。"

DAY 2 Field Day — Rural Jiangxi (Shangrao) 田野调查：江西上饶 Field

Objectives

Meet your real users. Watch them use the thing you built yesterday — the most honest feedback you will ever get.

Process

  • On the train: finalise your questions, assign roles, test Lark. Interview in pairs — one asks, one records
  • Arrival: school tour, icebreaker and group game, lunch (hosted by Zhong Gu)
  • Part 1: our students run "Where do you stand" for their rural peers
  • Part 2: Demo field-test — village students use your product while you observe and take notes
  • Part 3: knowledge challenge quiz
  • Part 4: Wish wall — "I hope AI can do ___ for our school." Photograph every single one
  • Home visits: one team per family, interviews only
  • On the train home: turn what you saw into product decisions

Mindset

We are here as listeners, not saviours. The real work today is listening.

今天的目标

见到真实的用户。看着他们使用你昨天做的东西——这是你能得到的最诚实的反馈。

流程

  • 火车上：确定访谈问题、分工、测试 Lark 录音。两人一组，一人问、一人记
  • 抵达：参观校园、破冰游戏、午餐（众谷主持）
  • Part 1：由我们的学生为乡村同学主持"你站在哪一边"
  • Part 2：Demo 实测——乡村同学真实使用你的产品，你在旁边观察记录
  • Part 3：知识挑战小竞赛
  • Part 4：心愿墙——"我希望 AI 能为我们校园做 ___"，每一条都拍下来
  • 家访：每组一个家庭，只做访谈
  • 返程火车上：把发现变成产品结论

心态

我们是倾听者，不是拯救者。今天真正的工作是听。

DAY 3 Product Design and Ethics 产品设计与伦理审计 PRD

Objectives

Leave with three things: a working GitHub team repo, a PRD (Project Requirements Document) with your team's Constitution built into it, and a clickable demo built from that PRD.

Process

  • Mini-lecture: what does the product development process actually look like in the tech industry today?
  • Set up GitHub: create your GitHub account and join your team's shared repository
  • Demo: what does a strong PRD look like?
  • Write your PRD: first on your own, then have AI review, refine and critique it
  • Ethics lecture (laptops closed): what is philosophy? Three lenses — consequences (Consequentialism), rights and duties (Deontology), virtue (Virtue Ethics) — each illustrated with case studies
  • Guided ethics review: individually run your own team's app through the Ethics Review Tool and generate your personal constitution
  • Merge into a team Constitution and append it to your PRD — it will guide the rest of your build
  • From PRD to demo: vibe code a clickable demo of your app idea

The most important sentence today

"A rule with no mechanism is a rule you will break."
Finding a violation in your own product is a win, not a failure.

今天的目标

带走三样东西：一个可用的 GitHub 团队仓库、一份写入了团队宪法的 PRD（产品需求文档），以及一个从这份 PRD 做出来的可点击 demo。

流程

  • 迷你讲座：今天科技行业里的产品开发流程到底是什么样的？
  • 搭建 GitHub：注册你的 GitHub 账号，加入你们团队共享的仓库
  • 示范：一份合格的 PRD 应该长什么样？
  • 写你的 PRD：先自己独立写，再让 AI 来审阅、打磨、挑刺
  • 伦理讲座（合上电脑）：什么是哲学？三种视角——后果（后果主义）、 权利与义务（义务论）、德性（德性伦理学）——每种都配案例讲解
  • 引导式伦理审查：用伦理审查工具 对自己团队的产品做个人版审查，生成你的个人宪法
  • 合并为团队宪法，并附加到你们的 PRD 后面——它将指导你们接下来所有的开发
  • 从 PRD 到 demo：用 vibe coding 做出一个可点击的产品 demo

今天最重要的一句话

"没有机制保障的规则，就是你迟早会违反的规则。"
在自己的产品里发现漏洞，是加分，不是丢脸。

DAY 4 From Demo to MVP: the Table Becomes a Backend 从 Demo 到 MVP：让表格成为后端 Agent

Objectives

Today you graduate from writing code yourself to directing an agent that writes it. The one thing that must work: your app reads stories from the backend and writes a vote back to it.

Process

  • Tech lecture: what are the two files that steer an AI agent? — CLAUDE.md and SKILL.md (not code — instructions); plus clone / commit / push, open source and licenses
  • Plan + schema, built live on a projected table: one row per record, one meaning per column, what is a primary key? An ID on every row; a second vote goes in a new row (one-to-many)
  • What is a smoke test? Connect to the backend and prove the pipe works before writing any product code
  • READ: stories appear in your app
  • Open-source shortlist: see what others have already built; clone a sample SKILL.md
  • Write a Skill that actually works, then build the glossary feature — this product's one AI feature
  • WRITE: cast a vote and watch a new row land in the table, live on the projector
  • Developer Log: what did the agent get wrong today? What did you change?

Your Constitution goes to work

Put it at the top of CLAUDE.md — the agent reads it before it writes a single line of code.

今天的目标

今天你从"自己写代码"升级为"指挥智能体写代码"。今天必须跑通的一件事： 应用能从后端读出故事，并把一次投票写回去。

流程

  • 技术讲座：指挥 AI 智能体的那两个文件是什么？——CLAUDE.md 与 SKILL.md（不是代码，是指令）；以及 clone / commit / push、开源与许可证
  • 计划 + 数据结构：在投屏的表格上现场建表—— 一行一个记录、一列一个含义、什么是主键？每行都要有 ID；投票放新的一行（一对多）
  • 什么是冒烟测试？连接后端，先证明数据管道是通的，再写任何产品代码
  • READ：故事出现在你的应用里
  • 开源清单：看看别人已经做过什么，克隆一份示例 SKILL.md
  • 写一个真正好用的 Skill，然后做出生词表功能——本产品唯一的 AI 核心功能
  • WRITE：投一票，看着新的一行实时出现在投屏的表格里
  • 《开发者日志》：今天智能体哪里错了？你改了什么？

你的宪法，现在开始生效

把宪法放进 CLAUDE.md 的最上面——智能体在写第一行代码之前就会读到它。

DAY 5 Make It Work, Then Make It Honest 先让它能用，再让它诚实 Agent+

Objectives

Morning: actually finish and harden the product. Afternoon: make it honest with its users, then attack it yourself.

Process

  • Tech lecture: what does testing and debugging actually look like? Reproduce → isolate → fix. Errors are normal, and readable
  • Finish the build, working against the done-bar checklist
  • What is a rubber-duck review? Say out loud what the code should do, then have the AI read it and find the gap
  • Ship gate: your live URL must open on a Zhong Gu classroom computer. Hard gate, before lunch
  • Tech lecture: why can't you trust an AI's explanation of itself? Ask it why it wrote something, and it will invent a plausible reason
  • Build the transparency panel: show what you actually stored, never a story made up afterwards
  • Turn your Constitution into code: the term must appear in the story, the count must be in range, nothing unapproved is ever shown
  • Red team: break your own Constitution. Swap products with another team. Log every failure as "broke Article N"

The best experiment of the week

The AI reports a "confidence" score on its own output. Go and test whether that confidence actually predicts when it's wrong. Mostly it won't — because confidence is a story the model tells you about its answer, not evidence.

今天的目标

上午：把产品真正做完、做稳。下午：让它对用户诚实，然后亲手攻击它。

流程

  • 技术讲座：测试与调试到底是什么样的？重现 → 定位 → 修复。报错是正常的，也是可读的
  • 把功能做完，对照验收清单逐条检查
  • 什么是橡皮鸭复查？先说清楚这段代码"应该"做什么，再让 AI 读代码，找出实际做了什么
  • 交付关卡：每组的网址必须能在众谷教室的电脑上打开（午餐前硬性通过）
  • 技术讲座：为什么不能相信 AI 对自己的解释？问它"你为什么这么写"，它会编一个听起来很合理的理由
  • 做透明面板：展示真实存下来的东西，而不是事后编的说法
  • 把宪法变成代码：生词必须真的出现在原文里、数量必须在范围内、未经审核的不许显示
  • 红队：攻破你自己的宪法。与另一组互换产品，失败都记为"违反第 N 条"

本周最好玩的实验

AI 会给自己的每条输出打一个"信心值"。去验证一下：它的信心真的能预测它有没有错吗？ 多半不能——因为那只是它讲给你听的故事，不是证据。

DAY 6 Test It, Freeze It, Tell the Story 实测、冻结、讲好你的故事 Workflow

Objectives

Test the product under real rural conditions, lock the version, then start shaping your pitch and your ethics case.

Process

  • Tech lecture (a look ahead): what's the difference between agentic and multi-agent? Live demo of Claude Code multi-agent — nobody wrote the order; it read the descriptions and decided who to call
  • What is a blind test? Swap products, throttle to Slow 3G, slow the CPU, shrink to a phone screen — then use it cold, with no walkthrough
  • Fix what genuinely blocks a real user, then CODE FREEZE — no new features after this
  • Lecture: how has AI developed over time?
  • What makes a strong pitch? Problem, user, product, proof, ask
  • Ethics case writing workshop (Marc Sanders Case Competition)

An honest closing

"That took four model calls to do something one agent could have done. More agents means more cost, more latency, and more places to fail. Real engineers use one until they can't."

今天的目标

在真实的乡村网络条件下测试产品，锁定版本，然后开始打磨你的路演故事与伦理案例。

流程

  • 技术讲座（前瞻）：Agentic 和多智能体有什么区别？ 现场演示 Claude Code 多智能体协作——没有人告诉它顺序，它自己读描述决定谁来做
  • 什么是盲测？互换产品，把网络限速到 Slow 3G、CPU 降速、切到手机屏幕，然后完全无人指导地使用
  • 修复真正会挡住用户的问题，然后 CODE FREEZE 代码冻结——之后不再加新功能
  • 讲座：AI 是怎么一步步发展到今天的？
  • 一场好的路演要具备什么？问题、用户、产品、证据、诉求
  • 伦理案例写作工作坊（Marc Sanders 案例大赛）

诚实的结尾

"刚才那件事用了四次模型调用，而一个智能体就能做完。更多的智能体意味着更贵、更慢、更多出错的地方。 真正的工程师，能用一个就不用两个。"

DAY 7 Demo Day Demo Day 路演 Ship

Objectives

Ship it in public. You walk away with a live app, a Developer Log and an ethics case — that is your portfolio.

Process

  • Guest speaker: ethics and education
  • Technical rehearsal: a full timed run on the frozen build, with a screen recording as backup
  • Judges' hands-on test: judges use your product themselves and give feedback
  • Demo Day showcase: 10-minute pitch + 5-minute Q&A
  • Awards: Best Technical Architecture ($1,000 seed fund) and Most Humanistic Care (Marc Sanders Case Prize pathway)

今天的目标

把作品公开发布出去。你带走的是：一个在线可访问的应用、一份《开发者日志》、一份伦理案例—— 这就是你的作品集。

流程
